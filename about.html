About Us

At Wooden Toys of Lego, we're passionate about providing children with high-quality, eco-friendly toys that encourage imaginative play. Our toys are made from sustainably sourced wood and are designed to last for generations.

Founded in 1977, we've been providing families with the best wooden toys for over 40 years. We believe that play is an essential part of childhood, and our toys are designed to spark creativity and imagination in children of all ages.
